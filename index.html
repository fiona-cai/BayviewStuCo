Frequently
Asked Questions

What is StuCo?

At Bayview, Student Council is a democratically elected governing council of the student body.

What does StuCo do?

We're in charge of:
• Managing all clubs, including finances and scheduling
• Assisting in school advertisement (announcements, google classroom grade forums)
• Planning school events, such as Spirit Events, to raise and enhance school spirit
• Promote student activity and voice

How often does StuCo meet?

Weekly on Wednesdays

How do I run for StuCo?

When election season comes around in May, join your grade Google Classroom and look for the application link!
